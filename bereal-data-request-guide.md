# How to Request Your Personal Data from BeReal

## Steps in the App

1. Open the **BeReal** app
2. Tap your **Profile** (top right)
3. Tap **Settings** (gear icon)
4. Tap **Help**
5. Tap **Contact Us**
6. Tap **Report a Problem**
7. Tap **Other**
8. Tap **Still Need Help?**
9. Tap **Select Topic**
10. Select **"I'd like to request a copy of my data"**
11. In the message box, paste the message below
12. Tap **Send**

## Message to Send

```
Hello,

I am exercising my right under Article 15 of the GDPR to request a copy of all personal data you hold about me.

Please provide this data in a portable format (e.g., JSON/ZIP) within the 45-day legal timeframe.

Thank you.
```

## What to Expect

- BeReal must respond within **45 days**
- You'll receive a ZIP file containing:
  - Your photos in WebP format
  - A JSON file with all your data and metadata
